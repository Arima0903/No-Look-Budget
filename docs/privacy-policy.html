Privacy Policy

English Version

✅

This app does not collect any personal information such as names or email addresses.

Data Storage

Only budget, expense, category, and settings data are stored locally on your device. No third party, including the developer, has any means to access this data. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.

Widgets

Home screen widgets use App Groups (on-device sharing) to display data. No data is sent externally.

Apple & Payment Information

In-app purchase transactions made through the App Store are managed by Apple Inc. The developer does not have access to any payment information.

Advertising (Free Plan Only)

If an advertising SDK is introduced in the future, this policy will be updated in advance, and your consent will be requested again.

Data Deletion

All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Sharing

Your data is never sold, shared, or provided to any third party.
